Getting started

Account & first business

Create an account with email and password or Google sign-in. After the welcome flow, choose a business type—it sets sensible defaults for categories, units, statuses, and custom fields. You can change all of that later in Settings.

You’ll need to add at least one location before finishing setup. Locations are where stock is tracked.

What Stockery focuses on

Stockery is built for inventory visibility: assets, quantities per location, movements, and history. It does not include billing, invoicing, barcode scanning, full accounting, or supplier purchase orders.

Help by topic

Dashboard

Your home screen shows business name, key metrics (totals, low stock, out of stock), status breakdown, idle items, stock flow trends, and recent activity. Use the FAB to add or remove stock quickly or create a new asset.

Assets

Browse with search, filters (category, status, location, custom dropdowns), sort, and list or grid view. Open an asset for summary, activity, related items in the same category, and files. Edit core fields and manage images from detail.

Stock & movements

Quantities are tracked per location. Use quick add/remove from the dashboard or asset detail. Movements (in, out, transfer, adjust) feed into trends and activity where the app connects them.

Activity, analytics & notifications

The Activity tab is your full timeline of changes. Analytics summarizes counts, idle assets, and breakdowns by status. Notifications cover things like low stock, out of stock, activity, and system messages—you can mark read or clear them from the app.

Settings & customization

Update business name and type, then manage categories (including parents), locations, statuses (with colors), units, and custom fields (text, number, date, dropdown, boolean). Theme (light/dark) lives in Settings too. You can delete your account from Settings when needed.

Offline & connectivity

The app caches data and can show cached content when the network is unreliable. If a full load fails and there’s no cache, you may see an error screen—try again when you’re back online.

Frequently asked questions

Can I change my business type after signup?

Yes. Defaults come from the type you pick at onboarding, but categories, locations, statuses, units, and custom fields stay editable in Settings—including changing business type from business setup/settings where the app exposes it.

Where are my photos stored?

Asset images are uploaded to secure cloud storage tied to your account and business, as implemented in the app (e.g. Supabase Storage). You manage them from each asset’s detail screen.

How do I reset my password?

Use the “Forgot password” flow on the login screen. You’ll receive email instructions to set a new password if your account uses email sign-in.

Does Stockery replace my accounting software?

No. Stockery is for tracking what you have and how it moves—not for invoicing, tax accounting, or full financial records.

How do I delete my account?

Open Settings in the app and use the delete account option. That removes your app data and account per the app’s implementation (including backend cleanup where configured).
